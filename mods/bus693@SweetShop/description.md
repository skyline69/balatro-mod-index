# SweetShop

Labels for your near-vanilla mods.

<img src="https://github.com/bus693/sweet-shop/blob/main/assets/original/example.png" width="644">

Vanilla rebalance mod screenshots look like regular games, but they play differently. This mod provides a subtle hint in the bottom left corner to people you share screenshots with.

List of current mods that this mod can label:

- [Rebalatro](https://github.com/lilaclila/Rebalatro)

**Mod authors**: If you would like to add your mod to the list, simply include the following line in your mod config `.json` file:

`"tags": ["vanilla_balance"],`

## Install

Any way you usually get your mods should work. If you are new to modding, I recommend [imm](https://github.com/frostice482/balatro-imm). For a minimalist setup, you can grab the latest release ZIP and copy it to your mod folder.

## Dependencies

This mod depends on lovely (`0.9.0` or newer) and steamodded (`1.0.0~BETA-1814a` or newer). I will consider removing the steamodded dependency if someone tells me about a vanilla balance mod that does not use steamodded.
