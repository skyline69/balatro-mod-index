A mod that adds a collection of jokers inspired by Genshin Impact and Honkai Star rail characters to the game, purely decided by characters I personally like or happened to have cool idea for.
They are designed to feel mostly aligned and fit in with vanilla content.

### Bugs, Feedback, or dicussion
I'm most likely to respond to either discord DM, or a message in the [Balatro discord thread](https://discord.com/channels/1116389027176787968/1443963518016684145)
