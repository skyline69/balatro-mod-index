# Subtractions

Do you want too many additions, but fear getting a specific joker from that one stupid mod you added?

This allows you to ban cards by clicking them in collection or a mod's additions tab.

- left click - ban that specific card
- right click - ban the entire current page
- middle click - ban the entire category (e.g. all jokers)

Will stop you from banning the default card for each Vanilla category.

_WIP and tested only with the specific stuff I play, if you ban something and it appears open an issue or such_
