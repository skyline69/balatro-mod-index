## It's THE Balatro Mod of All-time!

Bad Director is a mod that is heavily inspired by the idea of Misprint Joker from the base game and Love, and also a bunch of Vanilla-esque Balatro Mods:

Introducing:

## Misprinted-type Consumable!

It's a twist on the base consumables set by taking the idea of Misprint Joker and apply it to every sets of consumable, it's a very high risk, high reward! **(VERY UNSTABLE)**

## Sponsor! (Voucher-esque)

Now you can sign yourself a sponsor agreement to grant yourself a permanent stats up, whether it's Hands or Interest Cap! Be careful though, signing too many Sponsors can lead into some.. unforeseeable consequences.

## Misprinted Decks!

Beating a _certain_ Deck will now grant you the ability to Misprint every Vanilla Decks, and their effects are now different from what you know from the base game!
