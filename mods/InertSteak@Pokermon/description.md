A full content mod for Balatro, adding content based on Pokémon! There are over 450(!!) Pokemon Jokers that have been added alongside many other custom additions. 

Do you like Pokémon? Do you like Balatro? If you answered yes to either, then this mod is for you! Either by themselves as a completely new way to play (recommended), or alongside the classic Balatro jokers! 
You can't go wrong! This mod has a lot of effort and hard work put behind it, so I hope you'll enjoy!
