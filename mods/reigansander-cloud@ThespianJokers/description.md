# Thespian Jokers

Adds 5 custom Jokers: The Mason, The Fishmonger, The Prospector, The Thespian, and The Whittler.

## Requirements
- [Steamodded](https://github.com/Steamopollys/Steamodded) (required)

## Installation
Download and extract into your Balatro `Mods` folder like any other Steamodded mod.
