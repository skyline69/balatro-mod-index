# Vanilla Redux

A comprehensive Balatro rebalance mod that reworks and rebalances 75 Jokers, 2 Tarot cards, and 6 Spectral cards. Preserves vanilla feel while adding depth and variety

## Features

- **75 reworked Jokers** — rebalanced effects, scaling, rarities, and costs
- **2 modified Tarot cards** — The Hermit, The Wheel of Fortune
- **6 modified Spectral cards** — Ankh, Familiar, Hex, Immolate, Incantation, Ouija

## Requirements

- [Lovely](https://github.com/ethangreen-dev/lovely-injector) (latest version)
- [Steamodded](https://github.com/Steamodded/smods) (SMODS mod loader)
