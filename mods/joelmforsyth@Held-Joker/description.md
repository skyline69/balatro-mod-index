## Held Joker

Hold one joker from the shop (or booster packs) in a special reserve slot next to your consumables. The held joker doesn't count toward your joker limit — add it to your joker row for **free** whenever you have an open slot, or sell it for cash. Persists across rounds until you decide what to do with it.

**Features:**
- **Hold** — Buy a joker from the shop or grab one from a booster pack and stash it in the held slot
- **Add** — Move the held joker into your active joker row at no additional cost (requires an open slot)
- **Sell** — Sell the held joker for its sell value (unless it's Eternal)
- Preserves editions, stickers (Eternal, Perishable, Rental), and pricing
- Saved across rounds — your held joker is waiting for you every time you visit the shop
