Canned Laughter is a "completed" previously-definable-as-vanilla+ content mod starting from bare bones, blossoming out into its own concept, utilizing existing concepts and assets, remixed into their own new and engaging forms. 

This mod adds new items to almost every category in Balatro, aiming to make it a fuller experience overall using remixed assets from the base game, as well as unused/scrapped content, reworked for the current state of the game.
