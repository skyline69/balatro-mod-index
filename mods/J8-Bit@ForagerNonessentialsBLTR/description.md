# Balatro: Forager Nonessentials (v1.0)

**Forager Nonessentials** is a vanilla-adjacent mod aiming to add a decent variety of fun, fresh, and occasionally self-indulgent additions to Balatro. The features added by this mod are meant to compliment the main game, opening up new opportunities and different approaches to runs without completely overhauling the gameplay experience or overwhelming players with new content.

What to expect out of the mod:
- **60+** new Jokers!
- **8** new Decks!
- High-quality artwork and shader effects for various cards!
- Configurable alternate appearances for various Jokers!
- Cross-mod content!

## Installation

This mod requires [Lovely (**V0.8.0 or later**)](https://github.com/ethangreen-dev/lovely-injector) and [Steammodded **V1.0.0-beta-1016c or later**](https://github.com/Steamodded/smods). Once those are installed, download the [latest release](https://github.com/orangequilavaburst/ForagerNonessentialsBLTR/releases) and extract the `ForagerNonessentialsBLTR` folder into your mods directory.

## Configuration

Configuration options have been added to the mod to allow changing the appearances of various Joker artworks and appearances, as the initial concepts and visuals of some Jokers were not created for a general audience. Joker appearances can be changed in the Config tab of the mod - `Mods > Forager Nonessentials > Config`.
- `Original Art Direction`, when turned on, enables artwork and Joker concepts that were originally made for the mod, but may not appeal to a general audience.
- `No Deltarune Spoilers`, when turned on, changes the appearances and names of Jokers based on Deltarune Chapters 1-4, not including the cross-mod Jokers.

&lt;span style="color:red"&gt;**Recommended for streaming/recording videos for a general audience:**&lt;/span&gt; Turn off `Original Art Direction`.

&lt;span style="color:green"&gt;**Recommended for playing with the originally intended experience:**&lt;/span&gt; Turn on `Original Art Direction` and turn off `No Deltarune Spoilers`.

Buttons to discover and un-discover all Jokers exist on the config menu if you prefer going in completely blind or like seeing what you'd be getting up to. There are no unlock requirements for any of the added content; every Joker added by the mod is discoverable in a run and every Deck can be played with from the moment of installation.

### Mod Cross Compatibility

As of v1.0 of **Forager Nonessentials**, three mods will have special interactions when installed:
- [**The Undertale/Deltarune mod**](https://drive.google.com/drive/folders/19Tz5EzexavngDiuRV6rVp3KkDizFKz9D) - Adds 5 new Jokers
- [**ellejokers**](https://github.com/ellestuff/ellejokers) - Adds 5 new Jokers
- [**Ortalab**](https://github.com/EremelMods/Ortalab) - Adds 5 new Jokers

All three mods are not required to be installed at once in order for the content to be included; each respective mod's additions will show up individually. Cross-mod content can be enabled and disabled in the Config tab of the mod - `Mods > Forager Nonessentials > Config`.
