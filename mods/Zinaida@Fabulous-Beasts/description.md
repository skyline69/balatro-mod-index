# Fabulous Beasts
A Chinese mythology inspired content expansion mod for Balatro. Based on [Fabulous Beasts](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=GY3jg2heY30) (the manga and anime)
## Features
* 100+ custom Jokers
* Custom Boss Blinds
* Custom Booster Packs
* Custom Vouchers
* New rarities
* Secret interactions
* Hidden unlocks
* Secret synergies
## Boss Blinds
Includes:
* Wuxing-inspired blinds
* Zodiac-themed blinds
* Heavenly Stem inspired blinds
* Special challenge blinds

## Booster Packs
Includes:
* Cuisine Packs
* Jumbo Cuisine Packs
* Buffet Packs
* Beast Packs
* Heavenly Beast Packs

## Vouchers
Includes:
* Ancient Treasure
* Golden Mountain
* Utensils
* Cookware
* Pixiu Luck
* Divine Prosperity
* Vision
* True Sight
