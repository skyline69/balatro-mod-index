# Signalis Themed Balatro Tarot Deck

## Description
Changes the tarot cards to appear as the tarot cards from signalis.

Inspired by the mod from MrLightningRose [https://www.nexusmods.com/balatro/mods/556](https://www.nexusmods.com/balatro/mods/556)
Designs based on the cards seen in game and the additional designs from z0orbs64. All redrawn in Balatro's art style and format by me.

## Installation instructions
Extract folder into your Mods folder.

## Requirements
Requires [Steammodded](https://github.com/Steamodded/smods) and [Malverk](https://github.com/Eremel/Malverk)
